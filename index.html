Motivation
How it all started

Quran reciters so many times want to know where a certain verse belongs (surah and its index) and what message the verse delivers. We decided to solve this problem with a mobile app, where users can easily get the information of any verse from the holy book.

Features

We are currently building our MVP (minimum viable product)

Search For an Ayah
